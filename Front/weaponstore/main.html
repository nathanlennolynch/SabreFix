What is SabreFix?

SabreFix is a company specializing in the creation, upgrade, and repair of melee weapons. We offer services for maintaining and enhancing various types of weapons, including swords, axes, and other close-combat tools. Whether you need a blade sharpened, a hilt repaired, or an entire weapon upgraded, SabreFix has you covered. Additionally, customers can buy weapons through this platform, making it a convenient hub for all things melee-related.

WebSite Features

You can explore our collection of weapons and their specifications, easily buy the weapons you want and submit requests for upgrades or repairs to enhance your equipment.
